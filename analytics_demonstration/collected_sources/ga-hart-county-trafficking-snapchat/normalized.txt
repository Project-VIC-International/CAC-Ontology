Published: 2026-02-14

1) “Reed Cohen Skelton, 26… convicted of trafficking a 16-year-old female in Fulton County.”
2) “communicated with the victim using social media apps, including Snapchat…”
3) “Whether it’s Snapchat or Roblox or any other app… predators will find a way to communicate…”
4) “initiated online contact… for several months – requesting and sending sexually explicit content to the minor.”
5) “traveled to Cobb County to pick up the 16-year-old… transported the victim to a hotel in Fulton County… rented a room… engaged in commercial sex…”
6) “created and possessed digital files of the act…”
7) “paid the victim $500.”
8) “pleaded guilty… 1 count of Sexual Exploitation of a Minor; 2 counts of Trafficking of Persons for Sexual Servitude.”
9) “sentenced to 25 years… first 10 years… prison… remainder on strict probation.”
10) “required to register as a sex offender.”
11) “surrendered his nursing license as part of his guilty plea.”
